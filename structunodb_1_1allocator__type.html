Detailed Description

Pluggable allocator for ART trees.

All three function pointers must be non-null. ctx is forwarded to every callback and may be nullptr.

defer_dealloc is called when a node is removed and cannot be freed immediately (concurrent readers may hold pointers). For single-threaded trees the default calls destroy_callback immediately. OLC trees replace this with QSBR-based deferred reclamation.
